About Artemis Skys

A compact operations portal for small teams, focused on two critical workflows: who is working when and what inventory you have. This version stores data locally in your browser.

Key Features

  • Employee schedule maker with week view
  • Shift templates & quick assign
  • Inventory add/receive/issue with audit log
  • CSV/JSON export and import
  • Role-ready structure for future expansion

Tech Notes

  • Pure HTML/CSS/JS — no build step
  • LocalStorage for persistence
  • Modular code & simple state store
  • Easily swap auth for your backend
